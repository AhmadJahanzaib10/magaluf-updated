Ancient Egyptian Pharaoh Teddy Bear
Skip to the beginning of the images gallery

Exclusive to the British Museum, a teddy bear inspired by ancient Egyptian pharaohs.

The bear himself has been made from recycled fibres, including recycled plastic fibres and glass beans. His eyes are embroidered; a safer alternative to traditional plastic beads.

The bear is wearing the traditional striped nemes headdress, collar and heavy eye make-up typical of many of ancient Egypt’s pharaohs. The golden tones of his clothing are reminiscent of the richly decorated accessories which displayed the rulers’ wealth and power.

A vibrant ancient Egypt-themed gift for history-loving children.

  • Product Weight: 0.16Kg
  • T.P: 6.98
  • Dimensions: H23 x W18 x L15cm
  • Material: Recycled plastic fibres and recycled glass beads
  • Postage Weight: 0.20 Kg
